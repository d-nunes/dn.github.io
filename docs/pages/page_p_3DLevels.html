3D Level Design

Being in the Computer Science and Game Design course at DigiPen, I was only required to take one level design course. In it, aside from the PCG Roguelike project, we had to create a 3D level for a player to navigate. That particular assignment got me interested in general level design (player guidance, scene composition, environmental storytelling, etc.). So, when I was finally able to take electives for my Design requirement, I decided to take the 3D Level Design course, which is traditionally only available to pure Design students.
