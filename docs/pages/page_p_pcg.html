PCG Roguelike

Students in the Computer Science and Game Design program at DigiPen will eventually take a level design course that has students work on a roguelike that's procedurally generated.

The instructor actually provided an initial framework that had many features already in it. I didn't feel comfortable working with something mostly developed, so I created my entire project from scratch within Unity.
